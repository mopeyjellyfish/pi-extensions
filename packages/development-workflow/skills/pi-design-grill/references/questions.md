# Design-grill question bank

Select only the smallest relevant subset.

## Problem

- Who experiences the failure, in what concrete situation?
- What happens today and why is it unacceptable?
- What evidence distinguishes the problem from a preferred solution?

## Appetite

- How much time is this worth before reconsidering the approach?
- Which scope may be cut while preserving the core value?
- Which safety, quality, and compatibility floors are fixed?

## Solution

- What are the macro elements and how do they connect?
- What will a user observe when it works?
- Can a breadboard or fat-marker sketch remove ambiguity without prescribing implementation?

## Rabbit holes and no-gos

- Which unknown could consume the appetite?
- What narrow patch, constraint, or spike contains it?
- Which tempting adjacent work is explicitly excluded?

End with a short list of resolved decisions and remaining blockers. Do not substitute a score for evidence.
