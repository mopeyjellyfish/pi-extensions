# Pi Design Grill

Read [references/questions.md](references/questions.md), the current pitch, and relevant repository truth.

Ask one decisive question at a time. Prefer the Question tool when available; otherwise ask conversationally and wait. Continue from prior answers rather than restarting the interview.

Challenge:

- whether the Problem is concrete and worth solving;
- whether Appetite is explicit and meaningful;
- whether the Solution is rough but coherent rather than either vague or over-specified;
- whether Acceptance Signals are observable;
- whether Rabbit Holes have patches, constraints, or owners for decisions;
- whether No-Gos protect the appetite;
- whether the pitch is rough, solved, and bounded.

Label facts, inferences, assumptions, and unresolved decisions. Do not manufacture approval. Record blockers as evidence and request the pitch transition only when consequential ambiguity is resolved or explicitly accepted by the user.
